Force Racing is a Formula Student team from Kakatiya Institute of Technology & Science.

Established in 2009, Force Racing is a 60-member student team primarily from the Department of Mechanical Engineering. Each year, we design, manufacture, and compete with a formula-style race car in national and international Formula Student competitions.,

Our mission is to develop industry-ready engineers by integrating rigorous technical execution with structured business and project management practices. Alumni of the team have progressed into high-performance motorsport environments and diverse engineering sectors, including Formula 1 and engineering consultancy.
